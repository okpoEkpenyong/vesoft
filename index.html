About Us

Mission Strides has built a reputation that has spanned over 17 years. With a penchant for top-quality work and prompt delivery, we have won the hearts of many clients within the Calabar environs and even outside. Our mission is to be top on the list of global paint manufacturers, painting service providers and marketers. To achieve this, we are currently involved in research and development, training of our staff in world-class paint institutes as well as constantly standardizing our laboratories with SON and other relevant bodies
